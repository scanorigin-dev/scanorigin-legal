Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the ScanOrigin app collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Data Collection

ScanOrigin uses Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob to collect anonymous usage information and to display advertisements.

No personally identifiable information (such as name or address) is collected.

2. Device Access

Camera access is required only to scan product barcodes. No photos or personal data are stored.

3. Data Sharing

The data collected is processed by Google (Firebase, AdMob) in accordance with their privacy policies.

4. User Consent

A consent screen is shown on first launch. The user may refuse anonymous data collection.

5. Contact

For questions, please contact us at: scanorigin.app@gmail.com
